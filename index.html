Why we collect this information

MSHL Consulting & Projects Pty Ltd ("the Company") collects your name, surname and arrival time to keep an accurate attendance record for this workshop, for health and safety purposes and for our own administrative records.

What we collect

  • Full name and surname
  • Time of arrival (recorded automatically)

How it's used and protected (POPIA)

In line with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA), your information will only be used for the purpose stated above. It will not be sold, shared with third parties for marketing, or used for any unrelated purpose. Access is limited to the designated administrator of this event.

How long we keep it

Your information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for record-keeping related to this workshop, after which it may be deleted.

Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting the administrator using the details provided on this page.
